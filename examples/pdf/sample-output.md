# Quarterly Business Review: Q4 2025

## Executive Summary

Revenue grew 18% quarter-over-quarter, driven primarily by enterprise segment expansion. Customer acquisition cost decreased 12% due to improved marketing efficiency.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Q3 2025 | Q4 2025 | Change |
|--------|---------|---------|--------|
| Revenue | $2.4M | $2.8M | +18% |
| Active Users | 12,500 | 15,200 | +22% |
| Churn Rate | 4.2% | 3.8% | -0.4pp |
| NPS | 42 | 47 | +5 |

## Revenue Breakdown

### By Segment

- Enterprise: $1.8M (64% of total, up from 58%)
- Mid-Market: $720K (26% of total)
- SMB: $280K (10% of total)

### By Region

- North America: $1.96M (70%)
- Europe: $560K (20%)
- APAC: $280K (10%)

## Product Highlights

1. Launched v3.2 with real-time collaboration
1. Reduced average page load time from 2.1s to 0.8s
1. Added SSO support for enterprise customers
1. Shipped mobile app v2.0

## Challenges

- Engineering hiring slower than planned (4 of 7 roles filled)
- Enterprise deal cycles lengthening (avg 45 days, up from 38)
- Infrastructure costs growing faster than revenue (need optimization)

## Q1 2026 Priorities

- Close 3 remaining engineering hires
- Launch self-serve enterprise tier
- Reduce infrastructure cost per user by 25%
- Expand APAC sales presence
